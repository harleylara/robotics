- [Periodicity](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Periodic_function)

### Periodicity

<Equation>
    $$
    \sin(x)
    $$
    $\sin(x)$ and $\cos(x)$ are 2 $\pi$ periodic.
</Equation>

If we have a general **periodic** function $f(x)$, it may be $2L$ periodic.

<Equation>
    $$
    \sin(\frac{n\pi x}{L})
    $$
    Make $\sin$ and $cos$ a $2L$ periodic function.
</Equation>
