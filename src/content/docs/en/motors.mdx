## Winding

- oxygen free copper
- tight air gap

## Stator core

- laminated silicon steel (for core)
- low carbon steel (for flux ring)

## Magnets

Neodymium Magnets (NdFeB)
- Grade: N35, N42, N52, etc.
- Samarium-Cobalt (SmCo)
- Ferrite (Ceramic) Magnets

- strong magnets and tighter air gap decrease KV

- lower KV motors are more efficient: only due to practical manufacturing contraints
