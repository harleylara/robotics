## The Horrible Mess of Naming

- Before XXXX: was just `gazebo`
- In XXXX `Ignition Gazebo` was introduce: cohexistence of `gazebo` (the previous one) and `ignition`
    - `2018.03.02`: first appearance of the word `ignition` in Gazebo Forums [https://community.gazebosim.org/t/about-the-general-category/169](https://community.gazebosim.org/t/about-the-general-category/169)
- In XXXX `gazebo` -> `gazebo classic`
    - `2019.07.31`: first appearance of the term `Gazebo Classic` in Gazebo Forums [Use case of multiple “worlds” in an SDF - Gazebo Community](https://community.gazebosim.org/t/use-case-of-multiple-worlds-in-an-sdf/375/2).
`gazebo classic` EOL and 
- In 2022.4.6 `ignition` -> (`gazebo` or in short `gz`). see [A new era for Gazebo](https://www.openrobotics.org/blog/2022/4/6/a-new-era-for-gazebo) *"we're taking the opportunity to simplify things, for us and for you."*. [A new era for Gazebo - Gazebo Community](https://community.gazebosim.org/t/a-new-era-for-gazebo/1356)
