## Physical Interfaces

Universal Serial Bus (USB)
- USB 2.0 480 Mbps
- USB 3.0 5Gbps

| Feature            | USB 3.0   | GMSL           | MIPI CSI-2     |
|--------------------|-----------|----------------|----------------|
| Bandwidth          | 5 Gbps    | 6 Gbps         | 5 Gbps         |
| Cable length       | < 5 m     | 15 m           | < 30 cm        |
| Space requirements | High      | High           | Low            |
| Plug-and-play      | Supported | Not supported  | Not supported  |
| Develipment cost   | Low       | Medium to high | Medium to high |
