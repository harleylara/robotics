## Agents and Environments

<Definition term="Agent">
An agent is anything that can be viewed as perceiving its environment through sensors and acting upon that environment through actuators.

Source: Artificial Intelligent a Modern Approach - Chapter 2 section 1
</Definition>

**agent function** maps from any percept sequence to an action.

Just keep in mind that the term **agent** is just a analytical tool.

## Good Behaviour: The Concept of Rationality

<Definition term="rational agent">
A rational agent is one that does the 'right thing'.

Source: Artificial Intelligent a Modern Approach - Section 2.2
</Definition>
