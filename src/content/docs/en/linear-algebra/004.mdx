title: "Factorization into A = LU"
---

<Definition>
    $$
    \mE_{2,1} \mA  = 
    \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 0 \\
    -4 & 1
    \end{bmatrix}
    \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 1 \\
    8 & 7
    \end{bmatrix} = 
    \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 1 \\
    0 & 3
    \end{bmatrix} = \mU
    $$

    LU example.
</Definition>

This is elimination without row exchanges. The upper triangular $\mU$ has the pivots on its diagonal. The lower triangular $\mL$ has all l's on its diagonal. The multipliers $l_{ij}$ are below the diagonal of $\mL$.

<Definition>
    $$
    \mA = \mL \mU \\
    \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 1 \\
    8 & 7
    \end{bmatrix} =
    \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 0 \\
    4 & 1
    \end{bmatrix}
    \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 1 \\
    0 & 3
    \end{bmatrix}
    $$
    LU continuation.
</Definition>

<Definition>
    $\mA = \mL \mU$
    LU decomposition.
</Definition>
