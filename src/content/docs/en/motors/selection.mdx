## Collect Operational Requeriments

Motion objectives:
- Motion types: from the load view, is the load moving in a linar or rotary fashion
  - Linear: Horizontal or vertical
  - rotary
- Control types:
  - torque
  - position: relative position accuracy, absolute position accuracy (repeatability)
  - speed
- Motion variables
  - Maximum Velocity
  - Constant Time (time during constant operation)
  - Acceleration Time
  - Deceleration Time
  - Dwell Time (Time at zero speed)
- Load dynamics:
  - Does the load change during the duty cycle ?
  - Holding brake applied during zero velocity ?
- Thermal considerations
