## Preliminaries

Definition of quantities:
- $\vx_{k}$: state vector at time $k$
- $\vu_{k}$: control input vector at time $k$
- $\vm_{i}$: location vector of the landmark $i^{th}$
- $\vz_{ik}$: observation of landmark $i^{th}$ at time $k$ relative to the robot current pose
