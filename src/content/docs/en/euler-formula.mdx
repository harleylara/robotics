## Euler's Equation

"The Most Beautiful Theorem in Mathematics"

<Definiton>
    $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$
    Euler's Equation (formally Euler's Identity). *'The most beautiful theorem in mathematics'*.
</Definiton>
