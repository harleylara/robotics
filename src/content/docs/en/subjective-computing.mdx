Subjective computing is a combination of the topic **subjective statistics** and **scientific computing**. These two fields provide with the set of tools to solved computational problems with data.

<Definition
    term="Inverse problem"
    definition="The problem of retrieving information of unknown quantities by indirect observations."

For example [Pierre-Simon Laplace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pierre-Simon_Laplace) who estimated the mass of planets from astronomical observations, see [Memoir on the probability of the causes of events](https://www.york.ac.uk/depts/maths/histstat/memoir1774.pdf)
