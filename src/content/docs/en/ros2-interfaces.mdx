## Short About DDS

Communication paradigms:
- Publication/Subscription (asynchronous)
- Services (synchronous)
- Actions (asynchronous)

## ROS Interfaces

- Messages: used by "publishers" and "subscribers"
- Services: used by "service clients" and "service servers".
- Actions: used by "action clients" and "action servers"
