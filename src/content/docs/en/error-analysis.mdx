<Equation>
    $$
    x = x_{best} \pm \delta x
    $$

    Measurement of the value $x$.
</Equation>

where:
- $x_{best}$ best estimate of $x$
- $\delta x$ uncertainty or error in the measurement

<Equation>
    $$
    \frac{\delta x}{|x_{best}|}
    $$
    Fractional uncertainty.
</Equation>
