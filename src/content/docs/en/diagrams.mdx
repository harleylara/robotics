[kroki](https://kroki.io/) *"provides a unified API"* for most of the text-based tools.

Diagram and plots libraries:
- [D3js](https://d3js.org/)
- [JSXGraph](https://jsxgraph.uni-bayreuth.de/wp/index.html): Strong focus on mathematics

Calculators:
- [desmos](https://www.desmos.com/)
- [GeoGebra](https://www.geogebra.org/)

WYSIWYG-based:
- [tldraw](https://tldraw.dev/)
- [excalidraw](https://excalidraw.com/)
- [diagrams.net (previously draw.io)](https://www.drawio.com/). [wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diagrams.net)
